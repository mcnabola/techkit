How do I disconnect the ESP8266 board from my computer. Can I just unplug it or do I have to safely eject it like I do a USB stick. Thankfully the answer is NO you do not need to safely eject the ESP.

https://arduino.stackexchange.com/questions/4769/can-i-simply-unplug-the-arduino-usb-if-serial-communication-is-active Even if I am uploading a sketch over serial? No there is no issue Microcontrollers are good at abruptly stopping and starting.

As long as it is not writing to EEPROM or Flashing something it should not corrupt something generally.(Does the arduino IDE flash stuff onto the board?) It is best not to disturb it as it is uploading a sketch but after it is finished it should be fine to unplug.
